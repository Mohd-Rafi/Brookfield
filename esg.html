Environmental, Health & Safety Certifications

IGBC Green Campus and LEED Certification

The Indian Green Building Council (“IGBC”) has introduced Green Campus Rating System. The rating system primarily focuses on the common areas, utilities and landscaping provided in the campus.

The certification program addresses priorities, which include:

  • Water conservation
  • Waste management
  • Energy efficiency
  • Reduced use of fossil fuels
  • Health and well-being of occupants.

Under this program, we have received following certifications:

IGBC Green Campus

Gold Certification

Candor Techspace K1

IGBC Green Campus

Platinum Certification

Candor Techspace G2 & Candor Techspace N2

LEED Certified

Tower 5 in Candor TechSpace N1
