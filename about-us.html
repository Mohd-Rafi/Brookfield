Overview

Brookfield India Real Estate Trust is India’s only institutionally managed public commercial real estate vehicle. Sponsored by an affiliate of Brookfield Asset Management (“BAM” or “Brookfield”), one of the world’s largest alternative asset managers with over $540 billion assets under management, our goal is to be the leading owner and operator of high quality commercial real estate assets in key gateway Indian markets.

We are a leading owner of a diverse pool of high-quality real estate assets in India, generating an attractive total return for our unitholders derived from:
  • High quality assets generating a robust Current Yield supported by stable cash flows
  • Growth: Same-store growth (36% market rentals headroom); Identified organic pipeline
  • Governance & Experience: Decades of managing public REITs with highest global corporate governance standards

Sponsor

The Brookfield REIT is sponsored by Brookfield Asset Management, one of the world’s largest alternative asset managers with over $545B+ assets under management and a 120 -year heritage as owners and operators, invested in long-life, high-quality assets and business in more than 30 countries around the world.

Brookfield owns and operates an irreplaceable portfolio of iconic properties located in the world’s most dynamic markets. Brookfield acquires high-quality assets on a value basis and drives investment performance through active operational improvements.
