Sponsor

Overview

The Brookfield REIT is sponsored by Brookfield Asset Management, one of the world’s largest alternative asset managers with over $545B+ assets under management and a 120 -year heritage as owners and operators, invested in long-life, high-quality assets and business in more than 30 countries around the world.

Brookfield owns and operates an irreplaceable portfolio of iconic properties located in the world’s most dynamic markets. Brookfield acquires high-quality assets on a value basis and drives investment performance through active operational improvements.
