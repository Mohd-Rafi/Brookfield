Overview

We are proud to create the places people want to live, work, discover & connect.

Brookfield India Real Estate Trust (BIRET) is India’s only institutionally managed public commercial real estate vehicle. Sponsored by an affiliate of Brookfield Asset Management (“BAM” or “Brookfield”), one of the world’s largest alternative asset managers with over $540B+ assets under management, our goal is to be the leading owner and operator of high quality commercial real estate assets in key gateway Indian markets.

Portfolio

Candor TechSpace, Sector 21, Gurugram
Haryana, India

One of the most vibrant campuses in North India. Spread over 27.7 acres, Candor Techspace G2 is the largest office SEZ in Gurugram in terms of operational area.

  • Located in close proximity to Delhi Airport and superior social infrastructure
  • Only office SEZ in India with 5 entry and exit point
  • Being awarded withBureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE) 5 Star rating and as well as Indian Green Building Council (IGBC) Platinum rating certified.

Kensington, Powai
Mumbai, India

The only privately owned SEZ in Mumbai Metropolitan Region. Located in Powai, this campus is a part of 250 acres Modern Township.

  • 1.5 M sf Grade-A office SEZ complex

Candor TechSpace, Sector 62, Noida
U.P. India

Spread over 19.3 acres, Candor TechSpace (N1) is the largest IT park in the Sector 62, Noida micro-market. The office park is the most significant development in the micro-market.

  • Located in close proximity to Delhi and is connected to the rest of NCR by National Highway 9.
  • Central Plaza with a large amphitheatre, Food court and cafeteria
  • ISO 9000:2005,ISO 14000:2005 and OHSAS 18000:2007 certified campus

Candor TechSpace, Newtown, Kolkata
West Bengal, India

The largest campus style office development in eastern India. The office park spread over 48.4 acres, is a Grade-A office park, located in the New Town precinct of the Rajarhat.

  • 3.1 M sf of Completed office development with a wide range of amenities
  • Tenant engagement initiatives including sports activities and regular social events
